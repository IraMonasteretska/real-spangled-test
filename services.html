Services

Welcome to our services page, where we provide comprehensive real estate transaction services to our client’s. Our company specializes in buying, selling, residential property management, and investments

Buying services:

My buying services are designed to help my clients find that ideal property within their desired budget. With my extensive knowledge and expertise in all things real estate it is my duty and goal to walk you through every step of the home buying process. From getting you with the best lenders in the business, to sending you specified home searches matching your optimal criteria, being available for you, staying in constant contact, advocating for you above all and getting us to the closing table.

It is extremely important to me that you understand, and are comfortable, and confident in every decision you make. I will ensure timelines are met, proper documentation is in place, and that we get the best possible. I understand that purchasing not just a house, but your home is a significant investment, and I take my duty to serve and support you passionately.

Listing services:

Moving is always stressful, but I’m here to alleviate as much of that as possible when placing your home on the market. With my comprehensive awareness on current market conditions, and real estate expertise I will guide you through the entirety of selling your home.

I will put together a comparative market analysis to show you how I came up with the most competitive price point, as well as provide an estimated net proceeds sheet to give you different scenarios that support the current market. Professional photography and videography is always a necessity when marketing properly. I will assure the quality of marketing used to advertise your home, and maintain constant exposure to encourage the public eye.

Maintaining perpetual communication, and having your family’s best interest at heart, I will solidify a smooth transaction on your home sell.

Investing:

As a fellow investor myself, I know that I am always looking for the opportunity to obtain the best deal, and return on investment. So, I understand first hand how important that is. Whether you are looking for the best long term rental investment, or the most ideal house to flip I can assist you.

Green or seasoned investors I am here to walk you through the entirety of the process. I am well versed in property management, and can help gauge price points to obtain desirable rental criteria and rental rates. Also, with my broad scope of knowledge on fixer uppers, I can assist you with project estimates in a timely manner from licensed and bonded contractors that I know and trust.

Let's get started today on your journey of building wealth through investing!

Property management:

I expanded our growth into the property management field in 2017, after many requests from clients. It’s extremely hard to find someone you trust with your investment whether that be long term or short term.

It is our duty to ensure, and maintain a competitive rental rate, property condition, timely rent payments, and well vetted tenants. We perform quarterly inspections on our properties, provide well detailed reports, and also build that much needed relationship with all of our owners and tenants. We set the bar exceptionally high when it comes to how we conduct our business. We strive to be the best in the market setting ourselves apart from everyone, and reminding you through our hard work and dedication to your investment that you made the right decision in choosing us.
